Biting Your Toothpaste? Meet The New, Sustainable Way To Brush Your Teeth

ABOUT US

The Bite Toothpaste Bits are the brand’s hero product. Instead of the traditional toothpaste most of us are accustomed to, Bite puts all the cleansing power of toothpaste into pressed, bite-sized chewable toothpaste Bits. They look like small mints, each one the exact amount you need to brush your teeth.

There are both fluoride and fluoride-free options. The fluoride-free bits feature Nano-Hydroxyapatite, a mineral that helps strengthen and remineralize enamel, as the main ingredient. What’s not in each bit is as important as what is. You won’t find artificial flavors, sulfates, parabens, or harsh chemicals. You will find vegan, clean, cruelty-free ingredients — like xylitol, sodium cocoyl glutamate, calcium carbonate, and zinc citrate — that keep your mouth fresh and clean.
